### Core Research Focus
Our work focuses on the preservation of the skin's biological architecture.

* **Stratum Corneum Integrity:** Analysis of lipid leaching and protein denaturation in the presence of alkaline surfactants.
* **Molecular Precision:** Categorization of ingredients based on Dalton (Da) weight to ensure "top-down" renewal without trauma to living epidermal cells.
* **Stability Metrics:** Evaluation of antioxidant degradation (specifically L-ascorbic acid) into pro-oxidant dehydroascorbic acid.

**Why avoid Vitamin C (L-ascorbic acid)?**
Traditional Vitamin C is highly unstable in aqueous systems; its degradation into dehydroascorbic acid acts as a pro-oxidant, accelerating collagen cross-linking.

**What is the significance of molecular weight?**
OUMERE prioritizes ingredients based on Daltons (Da). If a molecule is too heavy, it sits on top; if it is too light or chemically enhanced, it may penetrate too deeply and cause inflammation. We focus on the optimal zonation for barrier repair.
